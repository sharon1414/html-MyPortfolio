SHARON'S FAVOURITE MOVIES IN 2023

MY CURRENT TOP 3 MOVIES

A WRINKLE IN TIME

This movie made for both kids and adults, brings a wonder that we "adults" loose as we get older. It also reminds of what is important and that love is the greatest power of all

PROFESSOR MARTESON AND THE WONDER WOMEN

This teaches that love doesn't have to look one way for it to be valid or wholesome

ALITA THE BATTLE ANGEL

We all have what it takes to succeed. sometimes we just have to go through the toughest battles to find that strength
